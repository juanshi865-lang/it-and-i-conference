Call for Papers

Information Technology and Implementation

IT&I 2024 invites original research papers, applied case studies, technical reports, and interdisciplinary work on the design, deployment, evaluation, and governance of information technology systems.

Submission Categories

Full research papers, short applied papers, system demonstrations, review papers, and case studies are welcome when they present clear technical contribution and implementation relevance.

Review Criteria

Submissions are evaluated for originality, method quality, technical clarity, reproducibility, implementation value, and fit with the conference scope.

Publication Format

Accepted manuscripts are published as digital proceedings records with title, authors, affiliation, abstract, keywords, citation metadata, and web-native full text.

Ethics and Originality

Authors should submit original work, identify prior publication status, disclose conflicts, and follow responsible research practices for data, software, and human subjects.
